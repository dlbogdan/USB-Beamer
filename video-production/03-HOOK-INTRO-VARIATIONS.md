# Hook & Intro Variations
**Purpose**: Test multiple opening hooks to find the most compelling  
**Target Duration**: 10-30 seconds  
**Goal**: Stop the scroll, create curiosity, promise value

---

## OPTION 1: Problem-First Hook (Recommended)
**Opening shot**: Frustrated face or messy cable situation  
**Duration**: 0:00 - 0:30

> "USB cables have a 5-meter limit. So what do you do when your Home Assistant server is in a closet, but your Z-Wave and Zigbee dongles need to be across the house? Run 20 feet of USB cable? Use sketchy extenders? OR..." 
>
> **[Beat, smile, show device]**
> 
> "...you put those USB devices on your network. Today I'm showing you USB-Beamer: turn a $35 Raspberry Pi into a network USB hub that makes remote dongles appear as if they're plugged in locally. WiFi setup with your phone, zero-config Home Assistant addon, custom 3D printed case—this is the complete package."

**Why it works**: 
- Immediate pain point identification
- Creates tension
- Promises a clean solution
- Specific use case (Home Assistant users will instantly relate)

## TESTING RECOMMENDATIONS

### Test Group A: Problem-First (Options 1, 6)
- Best for viewers actively experiencing the problem
- High emotional resonance

### Test Group B: Bold Promise (Options 2, 5, 8)
- Best for algorithm/browsing discovery
- Creates immediate curiosity

### Test Group C: Technical Depth (Options 3, 7)
- Best for established channel with technical audience
- Builds authority

### Test Group D: Story (Option 4)
- Best for personality-driven channels
- Creates personal connection

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Multi-Take Strategy
1. Record ALL variations (each is under 30 seconds)
2. Edit rough cuts with each hook
3. Show to 3-5 people unfamiliar with the project
4. Ask: "Would you keep watching?"
5. Pick the winner based on feedback

### Enhancement Ideas
- **Captions**: Add subtitles from word one (many watch without sound)
- **Music**: Start before dialogue, keep it energetic but not overpowering
- **Pattern Interrupt**: Use a sound effect or visual glitch at 0:03 to grab attention
- **Thumbnail Sync**: Make sure first frame matches thumbnail for consistency

### A/B Testing on YouTube
If you have multiple channels or can re-upload:
- Test different hooks on the same video content
- Compare CTR after 48 hours
- Double down on winner for future content
