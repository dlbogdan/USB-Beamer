## üí° Pro Tips

### Before You Start
1. **Test everything twice** - Demos that work once might fail on camera
2. **Back up footage immediately** - Don't lose hours of work to corruption
3. **Record room tone** - 30 seconds of silence helps with audio editing

### During Filming
1. **Slate each take** - Say "Take 1, Take 2" to make editing easier
2. **Record in segments** - Don't try to do the entire script in one take
3. **Get extra B-roll** - You'll use more than you think

### During Editing
1. **Cut ruthlessly** - If a section drags, cut it
2. **Watch with fresh eyes** - Take a day off before final review
3. **Get feedback early** - Show rough cuts to friends
